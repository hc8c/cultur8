Cultur8: sites about TV, film and book analysis

Why a webring?

Simply put, because it's getting harder and harder to discover original, human-created content online. Search engine services, and their AI derivatives, are geared around providing answers to simple or specific questions. Rather than pools of diverse, organic and free-flowing content, the web is now dominated by content that's been written FOR search engines, or to serve advertising, or grab user data. What about the deep-dive, niche, fascinating and occasionally utterly random content you used to be able to just stumble across? That's (hopefully) what this web collection will be about.

Credits:I'm relying on onionring to get this going. Thank you to the original creators!

Who should join?

  • Sites should be written in English.
  • Like it says on the tin, sites should offer original commentaries, analysis or theories of film, TV or books. It can be any or all of those.
  • Content shouldn't predominantly or only offer reviews.
  • Content should be written by humans: no AI generated or suggested articles.
